WHO WE ARE ?

Our family staunchly endeavours to eradicate ignorance and strives hard to attain gender equality. We promote symbiotic relationships and are highly motivated, ignited and enthusiastic towards our dreams, duly standing by our motto of "We Care, We Serve".

HOW WE STARTED

It all started on February 2nd, 2015 by an under-grad student Md. Akramuddin Ansari of NIT Rourkela as 'Helping Hands' with the goal of educating under-priviledged children.
By July 2015, our family increased to 70 helping enthusiasts as well as expanded in number of other critical concerns such as gender equality, women's safety and providing basic amenities to the poor.
With lot of financial support and donations pouring in from NIT students and the local people, Helping Hands was officially registered as BLESS 'n' BLISS on November 18th, 2015.
Now we are operational in 4 cities that are Rourkela, Jajpur, Ranchi, Bangalore and will continue to work hard for our causes.
