SAKSHAM

"Saksham" is an initiative to provide self-defence training classes for girls. Through this project, Experts teach self defence techniques to the girls. This project is to make girls conquer their fear of the current problems of the Society. We are always encouraged by Authorities of the Institute to organize such activities for taking a step forward in making women self-sufficient.

‘Don’t let anyone tell you weak because u r a women’ -mary kom. When we think about self-defense, we rarely if ever think of a woman defending herself. we know it was something we never thought about. After all, i have parents they will defend me if need be, right?

In India, the cases of gender violence are increasing and many of which go unreported or unregistered. This is because India is a place where women are taught not to get raped but men are not taught to not to rape. But for the moment, it is very important for women in India to learn self-defence for their own safety and survival. Let her wings get free,let her live life in her own way,let her make own rules ,let her enjoy a bit more just like him. For this she has to protect herself ,she has to stand for herself. For the safety of women and empowering for a better future we BNB club member are here with PROJECT SAKSHAM. It a training program of self defense for girl in NIT ROURKELA.

We aim at making each girl capable enough to protect herself. Mr Srat ku Das general secretary of Sundergarh district Wushu association will be demonstrating the martial arts along with lady self defense expert on 26 and 27 of October 2019 in NCC ground from __am onwards. Interested girls are requested to register themselves for Saksham. Let her not be considered as weak threat of society.
