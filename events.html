2020

RATION DISTRIBUTION DRIVE

Amidst the Global pandemic outbreak of novel Coronavirus, Bless'n'Bliss again took a step forword to provide essential items to the needy in Ranchi.

Bless'n'Bliss Ranchi in collaboration with NIFFT organized a Ration distribution drive in Ranchi. They distributed 110 packets of ration(groceries) in Nirmala Kusht Colony, Education Center(EC-1) of Bless'n'Bliss Ranchi. Bless'n'Bliss offers heartfelt gratitude to the faculty & staff members of NIFFT for their help & support. Bless'n'Bliss will always be with the Society in need. Our members are sincerely working round the clock in the city to help the people in need. Our deepest condolences to the deceased & warm prayers for those infected ones with the deadly virus. Everyone stay careful while moving out in emergency. Stay indoors, Stay safe.

SAKSHAM

The Bless'n'Bliss club organised a self defence training programme under the name of “Project Saksham” for girls in our campus premises on 26th, 27th October 2019 in three different sessions.

We proudly announce that the project was successfully c ompleted as more than 200 girls participated and learnt many different types of self-defence techniques that would help them at any moment of their life when they will be in trouble.

A lot of positive feedbacks were recieved by our dedicated members and participants were heard saying that they were grateful for they felt like they were prepared to conquer. Motivation of our team was enhanced to a great extent as we received appreciation from institute authorities for organising activities that took a step forward in making girls self-sufficient. B’n’B is always committed to help the needy, encourage the talent and serve the society.

CYCLOTHON 3.0
